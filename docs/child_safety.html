Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: July 03, 2026

Saini Uday is dedicated to providing a safe, secure, and respectful community app. We enforce a zero-tolerance policy against any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM), child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), and any conduct that threatens or exploits children.

1. Zero Tolerance Policy

We absolutely prohibit the uploading, sharing, transmission, or facilitation of any content related to:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or Child Sexual Exploitation and Abuse (CSAE).
  • Grooming, solicitation, or harmful behavior directed at minors.
  • Underage profiles or matrimonial registrations involving individuals under the age of 18.

2. Proactive Prevention & Moderation

To keep our platform safe for all users, we employ a combination of technological safeguards and manual review processes:

  • Age Gating: Registration is strictly limited to users aged 18 and older. Matrimonial registries undergo verification steps to ensure compliance with legal age requirements.
  • Content Moderation: Images and text uploaded to the community feed, matrimonial registries, and marketplace listings are screened using automated detection filters and manual reviews to identify inappropriate or illegal content.
  • Account Termination: Any user attempting to share, distribute, or solicit CSAE will face immediate and permanent ban from the application.

3. Reporting Content

If you encounter any content or behavior on Saini Uday that you believe violates these standards, exploits minors, or constitutes child abuse, please report it immediately:

  • Use the in-app Report User or Report Post flags.
  • Contact our dedicated safety response team directly at: saini.techsol@gmail.com

4. Reporting to Authorities

Saini Uday complies with all applicable reporting laws. In accordance with federal guidelines, we report all instances of suspected child abuse and exploitation to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and collaborate with law enforcement authorities globally to assist in prosecution.
