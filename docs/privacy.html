Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: name, email address, mobile number, profile photo, and community-specific details such as gotra, village, occupation, and location. We also collect device identifiers for push notifications (FCM token), app usage data, and content you post (feed posts, marketplace listings, service listings, matrimonial profile).

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to: authenticate and manage your account, display your profile in the member directory, enable community features (feed, marketplace, services, matrimonial), send transactional and service notifications (OTP, order updates, booking confirmations), moderate content for safety, and improve the app experience.

3. Notifications

We send push notifications for OTPs, order/booking updates, community announcements, and messages. You can disable notifications at any time from your device settings. Essential notifications (OTP for login) cannot be disabled while using OTP login.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your profile information is visible to other registered members of the community. We share data with service providers (email delivery, SMS, Firebase/FCM for push notifications) only as needed to operate the app. We may disclose data to comply with legal obligations.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. Deleted accounts have their personal data removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law. OTP records are purged nightly after 24 hours.

6. Your Rights

You can view and update your profile at any time via the app. You can request account deletion by contacting us at the support email. You may request a copy of your personal data by reaching out to the admin.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes, API communication uses HTTPS/TLS, and JWT tokens are short-lived with refresh rotation. We do not store plain-text passwords or payment card data.

8. Children

The app is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via the app or email. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at: saini.techsol@gmail.com
